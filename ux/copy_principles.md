# Copy Principles

- Sound calm, specific, and local.
- Prefer direct utility over hype.
- Explain trust cues in plain language.
- Never imply that a suspicious listing is safe.
- Use active verbs: `Save`, `Apply`, `Follow`, `Report`, `Verify`.
- Make time visible: `Posted 18m ago`, `Story ends in 3h`, `Response due today`.
- Treat job seekers with dignity even when they have no polished CV.
