# Prompt #05 Information Architecture

The new IA is organized around movement, not modules. The primary layer is the delta stage: summary metrics plus ranked change events. The second layer is the live opportunity layer: urgent stories and ranked jobs. The third layer is support infrastructure: saved searches, alerts, employer proof, reporting, and story creation. Mobile prioritizes this as a feed. Desktop redistributes it into left rail, main feed, and sticky right rail so the surface feels operational instead of stretched.
