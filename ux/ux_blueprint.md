# UX Blueprint

## Design Principle

Build a **career utility surface with story-speed**, not a social app with jobs sprinkled on top.

## End-to-End Flow Summary

| Flow | User Goal | Friction Points | Trust Signals | Mobile-First Behavior |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| First-time onboarding | Get value in under a minute | Too many fields, early CV demand | Why each field matters, skip labels, data-use note | One-thumb cards, progress chips, sticky continue CTA |
| Language choice | Feel locally welcome fast | Language lock confusion | Easy switch, mirrored labels, simple wording | First screen, large tap targets |
| Seeker profile | Tell the app what matters | Shame around weak CV or experience gaps | Template help, optional fields, profile-completeness tied to benefits | Stepper with single-task screens |
| Employer verification | Prove legitimacy and urgency | Upload friction, fear of moderation delays | Badge ladder, clear review ETA, why verification unlocks reach | Phone-friendly photo capture, progress state |
| Home feed | See what changed since last visit | Noise and duplicate jobs | Freshness stamps, source provenance, trust badges | Cards optimized for fast vertical scan |
| Story reel | Scan urgent updates fast | Cringe risk, irrelevant stories | Role tags, company badges, direct actions | Horizontal reel with compact chips |
| Job detail | Decide whether to save or apply | Hidden salary, unclear legitimacy | Salary disclosure, last verified time, response-speed stats | Sticky bottom action bar |
| Apply flow | Move quickly without dropping off | CV upload, long forms | Quick apply defaults, profile reuse, external apply warning | Large tap zones and upload fallback |
| Candidate intro story | Present yourself in a credible way | Social anxiety, low confidence | Templates, preview, role targeting, expiry clarity | Camera-first with text-safe overlays |
| Employer urgent story | Signal urgency and fill roles fast | Low-quality employer media | Verification badge, role count, shift time, apply CTA | Template-first creation |
| Reporting / abuse | Flag suspicious content safely | Fear of retaliation or wasted effort | Anonymous reporting, clear categories, visible action states | Bottom sheet with one-tap reason selection |
| Status tracking | Know if effort is going anywhere | Silence after applying | Viewed / shortlisted / invited states, response timer | Compact status rail in profile and cards |

## Non-Negotiable UX Rules

- The home feed always answers: **what changed since I last checked?**
- Stories always answer: **what urgent action should I take now?**
- Empty states always propose a next move: widen radius, add role, follow employers, create intro story, or enable alerts.
- Candidate media never appears without role context and trust context.
