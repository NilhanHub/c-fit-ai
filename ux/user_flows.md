# User Flows

## Seeker Onboarding

1. Choose language.
2. Pick role families.
3. Pick preferred areas and commute radius.
4. Pick experience level and education.
5. Set salary floor and alert preference.
6. Land directly in the fresh feed with a prebuilt first saved search.

## Employer Verification

1. Enter company details and work email.
2. Verify domain or code.
3. Upload supporting proof if needed.
4. Receive provisional or full badge.
5. Create jobs and urgent stories after verification.

## Candidate Intro Story

1. Pick a template such as `3 Reasons to Hire Me`.
2. Record or upload media.
3. Add role target, language, and skills.
4. Preview trust-safe overlays.
5. Publish for 24 hours.

## Employer Urgent Story

1. Choose story type: urgent role, walk-in, hiring burst, shift fill.
2. Attach job link or create quick job stub.
3. Add timing, location, salary, and deadline.
4. Publish with badge and CTA.

## Report Flow

1. Tap report on job or story.
2. Pick reason.
3. Add optional note or evidence.
4. Receive confirmation and case state.
