# Final Wedge Decision

## Winner

**Build a trust-first freshness engine for Colombo jobs, then layer in exact-24-hour urgent stories that accelerate real job actions.**

## Product Name for This Build

**Colombo Jobs Pulse**

## What This Means in Plain Language

- The core product is **not** a generic social feed.
- The home experience is a **fresh verified jobs pulse** with urgency, response visibility, and trust cues.
- The story system exists to compress action around:
  - urgent hiring now
  - walk-in interview windows
  - shift or batch openings
  - candidate intro templates such as **“3 Reasons to Hire Me”**

## Why This Wins

- `FACT` Colombo has enough inventory density to support freshness-based checking. Source: `ikmanjobs-colombo-2026`.
- `FACT` Sri Lanka already has both professional-platform behavior and short-form/social behavior at scale. Sources: `datareportal-digital-2026-sri-lanka`, `linkedin-app-store-2026`.
- `FACT` Scam and trust issues are real enough that a trust layer is part of the wedge, not a hygiene feature. Source: `labour-ministry-ml-2025-03`.
- `INFERENCE` Users will re-open for things that may have changed right now, not for passive browsing.
- `INFERENCE` Stories are only valuable when they are job-relevant, time-bound, and deep-linked to application or status actions.

## Backup If the Story Layer Underperforms

Pivot the product emphasis to **response-speed and application tracking** while keeping stories only for employer-side urgent hiring bursts and walk-ins.

## Decision Constraints

- Candidate-created media must stay tightly templated and job-relevant.
- The platform must never reward empty posting volume.
- Trust badges, source provenance, and report flows are mandatory from v1.
- 20x/day should be targeted at **power users in high-intent periods**, not used as a universal assumption.
