# Colombo Jobs Pulse Market Map

## Market Truth

- `FACT` Sri Lanka still has a meaningful youth-employment problem: the Department of Census and Statistics reported **20.8% unemployment for ages 15-24 in Q2 2025**, far above the overall unemployment rate. Source: `dcs-lfs-q2-2025`.
- `FACT` The audience is digitally reachable enough for a mobile-first product: DataReportal reported **29.3 million cellular connections, 12.4 million internet users, and 8.20 million social identities in January 2025**, with even higher digital reach by late 2025. Sources: `datareportal-digital-2025-sri-lanka`, `datareportal-digital-2026-sri-lanka`.
- `FACT` Sri Lanka’s youth cohorts are digitally stronger than the national average: DCS computer-literacy data for 2025 H1 shows **75.6% computer literacy for ages 15-19**, **74.6% for ages 20-24**, and **78.6% for A/L and above**. Source: `dcs-computer-literacy-2025-h1`.
- `FACT` Android dominates local mobile usage, which supports an Android-first mobile web launch and low-end-device testing bias. Source: `statcounter-mobile-os-sri-lanka-2026-01`.
- `FACT` Trust is a real pain, not a theoretical one: Sri Lanka’s Ministry of Labour issued **Notice ML/2025/03** warning that fake part-time job ads were circulating on social media using ministry branding and false income claims. Source: `labour-ministry-ml-2025-03`.

## Current Colombo Job-Seeker Journey

1. `FACT` Discovery is fragmented across job portals, social media, messaging, walk-in/interview notices, and personal referrals. Sources: `topjobs-app-store-2026`, `ikmanjobs-sri-lanka-2026`, `xpressjobs-recruiter-features-2026`, `linkedin-app-store-2026`.
2. `INFERENCE` Young seekers check repeatedly because information changes quickly, not because existing products are habit-forming on their own.
3. `FACT` Colombo inventory is dense enough to support a freshness wedge: ikman exposes **4,692+ Colombo listings** and **10,000+ Sri Lanka-wide listings**. Sources: `ikmanjobs-colombo-2026`, `ikmanjobs-sri-lanka-2026`.
4. `FACT` Recruiter-side urgency already exists for walk-ins, high-volume hiring, and CV-less recruiting. XpressJobs explicitly markets **walk-in SMS**, **CV-less apply**, **ATS**, and **WhatsApp outreach**. Sources: `xpressjobs-recruiter-features-2026`, `xpressjobs-package-details-2026`.
5. `INFERENCE` The biggest user pain is not discovering that jobs exist; it is deciding whether a vacancy is real, still fresh, worth the effort, and likely to receive a response.

## Strongest First Wedge Segments

| Segment | Why It Matters | Why It Can Form Habit |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Early-career office and BPO candidates in Colombo | High vacancy churn, strong mobile reach, low switching cost | Fresh jobs, status changes, interview invites, urgent shifts |
| Final-year students and recent graduates | High anxiety, active search, poor signal quality today | Saved-search alerts, templated intro stories, employer bursts |
| High-volume hiring categories such as customer support, sales, cashiers, and call centers | Recruiters need speed, many candidates lack polished CVs | Walk-in and same-week hiring windows create repeated-check urgency |

## Incumbent Map

| Incumbent | What It Does Well | Where It Is Weak |
| --- | --- | --- |
| LinkedIn | Professional identity, network graph, alerts, content, Easy Apply | Too broad, too noisy, low local urgency, weak walk-in and local trust cues |
| topjobs | Large Sri Lanka-focused inventory and employer reach | Inventory-led rather than freshness-led; weak status visibility from seeker perspective |
| ikmanJOBS | Large volume, strong location/category breadth, accessible marketplace feel | Quality variance and mixed-trust environment from classifieds DNA |
| XpressJobs | Strong recruiter tooling, CV-less flows, ATS, WhatsApp, walk-in SMS | Seeker-side product feels more process-heavy than habit-native |
| Oh! Jobs LK | Licensed-company positioning, daily reports, direct messaging, rewards | Rewards are not enough to sustain trust or urgency alone |

## What Can Realistically Drive Repeated Checking

- `FACT` Frequent local hiring formats already exist, especially walk-in and high-volume recruitment. Sources: `xpressjobs-recruiter-features-2026`, `labour-ministry-job-fair-2025`.
- `FACT` TikTok and LinkedIn both have meaningful reach in Sri Lanka, which supports a hybrid of professional utility plus short-form behavior. DataReportal estimates **6.79 million TikTok users aged 18+** and **2.90 million LinkedIn members** in Sri Lanka in late 2025. Source: `datareportal-digital-2026-sri-lanka`.
- `INFERENCE` Repeated checking is realistic when the user believes one of four things may have changed since the last open:
  - a new verified high-fit job appeared
  - an urgent hiring window or walk-in slot appeared
  - an employer interacted with the user or updated status
  - a trusted story surfaced that is directly actionable

## Frequency Reality

- `INFERENCE` **5x/day** is realistic for activated users with saved searches and alerts.
- `INFERENCE` **10x/day** is realistic for users in active search weeks, especially around interviews, walk-ins, and application-status uncertainty.
- `INFERENCE` **20x/day** is realistic only for **power users and urgent cohorts**, not for the median user. The product should be built to create that behavior in high-intent windows, not assume it universally.
