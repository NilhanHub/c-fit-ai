# Jobs To Be Done

## Seeker JTBD

- Help me find **fresh, real, relevant jobs** without wasting time on stale or suspicious listings.
- Help me react quickly when a recruiter or employer is actually moving.
- Help me present myself fast even if my CV is weak or unfinished.
- Help me understand whether an employer is real, responsive, and worth my effort.

## Employer JTBD

- Help me fill urgent junior roles fast with candidates who actually fit.
- Help me communicate urgency, shift timing, walk-in logistics, or same-week hiring clearly.
- Help me screen candidates without drowning in irrelevant applications.

## Product Constraint

- Every major feature must improve either **freshness, trust, response visibility, or conversion speed**.
