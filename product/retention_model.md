# Retention Model

## What Is Permanent

- Seeker profile
- Employer profile
- Job postings
- Saved searches
- Applications
- Trust history
- Reports and moderation outcomes

## What Is Ephemeral

- Employer urgent-hiring stories
- Walk-in interview stories
- Candidate intro stories
- Campus or institute hiring-burst stories
- Some digest and reminder surfaces

## Retention Drivers

- Fresh jobs since last visit
- Status movement after applying
- Employer follow activity
- New urgent stories from trusted sources
- Better matching from repeated saves and applies

## 20x/Day Reality Statement

`INFERENCE` 20x/day is realistic for a minority of high-intent users and during high-urgency hiring windows. It is not realistic as the average behavior across the full user base, and the product should be designed around that truth.
