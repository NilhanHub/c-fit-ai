# Habit Loop

## Core Loop

1. User sets up role, location, language, salary, and notification preferences.
2. System detects fresh high-fit jobs, urgent employer stories, or application-status changes.
3. User opens the app to check what changed.
4. User saves, applies, follows an employer, or posts a templated candidate story.
5. System learns from those actions and tightens the next wave of alerts and feed ranking.

## Why People Return

- `5x/day`: check the fresh feed, scan stories, save jobs, and review employer responses.
- `10x/day`: active searchers re-open after alerts, before commute windows, and around interview or walk-in timelines.
- `20x/day`: power users do this when they are in a narrow hiring window, waiting on outcomes, or chasing same-week hiring bursts.

## Anti-Noise Rule

- No reward loops for generic posting.
- No public popularity metrics on candidate stories.
- No algorithmic reward for “engagement” without job relevance.
