# Prompt #05 Delta Experience

The product now treats "since your last visit" as the interface primitive. A visit can surface new jobs, new salary disclosure, trust upgrades or downgrades, employer activity, shortlist movement, walk-in openings, closing windows, and story go-live/expiry changes. These deltas appear in the summary metrics, the Move Now queue, job cards, job detail, alert queue, and story order so repeat checking feels justified rather than hoped for.
