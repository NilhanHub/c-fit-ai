# Model Boundary

## Decision Question

What AI offers are most likely to fit Colombo demand, through which segments, under what assumptions?

## v0 Geography

- `FACT`: v0 is Colombo District.
- `INFERENCE`: local heterogeneity is represented through broad area clusters rather than ward-level geography.

## What B2C Means Here

B2C means individuals embedded in households whose demand is shaped by:

- age and education
- livelihood status
- household context
- digital and payment readiness
- budget and trust sensitivity
- need-domain intensity

## What B2B Means Here

B2B means Colombo firms and buying centers whose demand is shaped by:

- sector and size
- formality and digital maturity
- workflow complexity
- customer interaction intensity
- payment cycle and admin burden
- ability to pay
- procurement behavior

## What Counts As An AI Offer

An AI offer is a concrete publicly stated product or productized service with:

- an identifiable vendor
- a specific workflow or problem solved
- a clear target market
- a usable evidence source

Vague “AI transformation” consulting claims do not qualify for this v0 corpus.
