# Question Definition

## Primary Question

Which India-first AI offers look strongest for Colombo District across B2C, B2B, and mixed-market scenarios, and why?

## Definitions

### Fit

Fit is a decomposable score that combines:

- problem intensity in a segment
- ability or willingness to adopt
- implementation friction
- go-to-market reachability
- likely retention or expansion path

### Demand

Demand means not only latent need, but also a plausible path to reach, activate, and retain the buyer or user inside Colombo conditions.

### Adoption Likelihood

Adoption likelihood is the model’s inferred probability of a segment trying and sticking with the offer, given the segment’s constraints and the offer’s demands.

### Commercial Attractiveness

Commercial attractiveness adds a market-side view:

- ability to pay
- channel practicality
- likely sales friction
- repeat usage potential

## Expansion Definition

Urban Sri Lanka expansion means a scenario that broadens the opportunity beyond Colombo while assuming somewhat lower budgets, lower digital readiness, and more uneven infrastructure than Colombo District.
