# Non-Goals For Prompt #02

- not a calibrated digital twin
- not a neighborhood-accurate commuter simulation
- not a 1000-offer scraping system
- not a validated forecast of actual revenue or adoption
- not a generic “AI market size” presentation
- not a polished dashboard-first product

## Anti-Bloat Rule

Prompt #02 models a disciplined set of consumer and business demand segments. It does not attempt to represent every possible Colombo persona, every firm subtype, or every AI category at full resolution.
