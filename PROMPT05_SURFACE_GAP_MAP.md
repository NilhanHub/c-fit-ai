# Prompt #05 Surface Gap Map

## Surface-level issues

- Softness was too uniform.
- Cards had similar density and importance.
- CTA language was serviceable but not sharp enough.
- Story cards lacked countdown and media-forward identity.
- Desktop lower-left space felt empty and confidence-lowering.

## System-level issues

- No shared event model made "since your last visit" feel decorative.
- Trust changes, salary changes, and risk changes were not integrated into ranking or alerts strongly enough.
- Saved searches and alerts did not visibly predict why the user should come back.
- Composers existed, but they did not feel like first-class actions.

## Rebuild priorities

1. Make delta/change visible everywhere.
2. Sharpen the feed hierarchy.
3. Make trust legible at the moment of action.
4. Turn stories into an urgent media layer.
5. Make desktop look intentional and mobile look feed-first.
