# Prompt #05 Hard Questions

## What looked good but was actually weak?

The prior version’s biggest illusion was compositional calm. It looked premium, but the feed did not visibly earn repeat checking behavior strongly enough, especially on desktop.

## What was over-spacious?

The old desktop canvas, the quiet detail card, and the low-energy right-lower utility stack.

## What lacked urgency?

Stories, alerts, and the "what changed" promise.

## What felt too much like a prototype?

The prior composer widgets, passive alert cards, and the trust explanation block as an appendix.

## What feels materially stronger now?

The delta stage, story reel, desktop layout, trust placement, job hierarchy, and action surfaces.

## What still remains too weak for launch?

Real notification delivery, persistence, moderation tooling, and authenticated employer workflows.

## What UI behaviors now better support frequent checking?

Visible delta ranking, safe-mode change surfacing, salary/trust shifts, shortlist/deadline movement, and urgent story countdowns.
