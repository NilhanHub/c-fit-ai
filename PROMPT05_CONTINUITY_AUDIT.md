# Prompt #05 Continuity Audit

## What existed before the rebuild

- Prompt #03 already established the winning wedge: freshness-first jobs, urgent stories, salary transparency, trust, and response visibility.
- `apps/web` already contained a working mobile-first seeded React app with onboarding, feed, job detail, composers, and reporting.
- Existing screenshots proved the concept, but they also exposed a gap between "smart MVP" and "high-frequency operating system."

## What stayed true

- Colombo-first early-career focus.
- Bilingual onboarding and interface support.
- Stories remain job-relevant rather than social-noise.
- Trust, salary visibility, and moderation remain core to the product.
- "Since your last visit" remains the central retention thesis.

## What changed in Prompt #05

- The app moved from a soft stack of modules to a pulse-driven operating surface.
- Delta events now drive summary, urgency, and feed behavior.
- Stories became a distinct media layer instead of polite information cards.
- Trust moved into cards, stories, alerts, and detail instead of living mainly in explanation copy.
- Desktop now uses a real three-zone layout. Mobile prioritizes the feed before secondary rails.

## Continuity risks watched during the rebuild

- Do not lose calmness while increasing urgency.
- Do not let the story layer become generic social UI.
- Do not hide trust behind a large help block again.
- Do not let desktop become stretched mobile.
