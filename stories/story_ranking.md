# Story Ranking

## Ranking Inputs

- Story freshness
- Verification strength
- Role match
- Geography match
- Employer response history
- Story actionability
- Prior negative reports

## Boost Rules

- Boost verified urgent-hiring stories with salary, timing, and location filled in.
- Boost walk-in stories close to event time for relevant users.
- Boost candidate stories only when profile completion and role match are high.

## Suppression Rules

- Suppress stories with weak identity, suspicious claims, or repeated negative feedback.
- Cap candidate-story frequency to avoid feed pollution.
