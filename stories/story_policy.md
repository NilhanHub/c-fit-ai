# Story Policy

## Allowed

- Job-specific hiring updates
- Interview or walk-in logistics
- Candidate proof points tied to real work goals
- Salary or shift disclosure
- Skills, language, or availability summaries

## Not Allowed

- Generic lifestyle or entertainment posting
- Misleading income claims
- Unverified financial asks
- Harassment, hate, sexualized content, or unrelated memes
- Candidate doxxing or exposing sensitive personal documents

## Report Reasons

- Fake job
- Scam or payment request
- Misleading salary or conditions
- Harassment or abuse
- Irrelevant / not job-related
- Privacy violation

## Moderation States

- Live
- Limited distribution
- Hidden pending review
- Removed
- Account escalated
