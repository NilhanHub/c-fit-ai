# Verification System

## Badge Ladder

- Unverified
- Provisional employer
- Verified employer
- Trusted fast-response employer

## Verification Inputs

- Domain email
- Business registration or equivalent proof
- Office or branch location proof
- Recruiter identity proof
- Response and report history

## Candidate Trust Signals

- Completed profile
- Skills and language fields
- Prior employer interactions
- Templated intro story completion

## Badge Effects

- Higher distribution
- Better trust placement in cards and stories
- Access to urgent-story formats
