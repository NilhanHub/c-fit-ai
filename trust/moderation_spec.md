# Moderation Spec

## Goals

- Keep the product useful, credible, and app-store-safe.
- Remove scams fast without freezing legitimate employers.

## Logic

- Pre-publish checks run on every job and story.
- High-risk content is blocked automatically.
- Medium-risk content is published with reduced distribution or queued for review.
- User reports can move content into review even after publication.

## Escalation

- First suspicious event: warning or temporary hold
- Repeat events: account throttle
- Severe scam indicators: immediate suspension plus evidence retention

## Abuse Throttles

- Story posting caps
- Rapid repost limits
- Report-spam limits
- Device and phone verification for repeat offenders

## App-Store Readiness

- Clear report flow
- Clear privacy policy for media
- Human review path for harmful content
