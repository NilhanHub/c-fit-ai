# Fraud Pattern Library

## High-Risk Signals

- Upfront fee requests for interviews, uniforms, deposits, or training
- Ministry or government impersonation
- WhatsApp-only applications with no company identity
- Unrealistic salary bands relative to role and location
- Reused phone numbers across multiple fake brands
- Recycled media or copied descriptions across unrelated listings

## Medium-Risk Signals

- Missing company website or domain mismatch
- Pressure to move off-platform immediately
- Job title and job description mismatch
- Repeated edits to salary or location after publication

## Product Response

- Warn
- Limit distribution
- Block publication
- Escalate to manual review
