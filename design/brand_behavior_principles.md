# Brand Behavior Principles

- Visual personality: warm Colombo light, pale premium surfaces, sharper contrast at moments of urgency.
- Interaction personality: direct, fast, and low-friction. Important actions should feel one tap away.
- Motion personality: meaningful and limited. Countdown, fresh movement, and reveal rhythm matter more than decorative animation.
- Trust personality: explicit, operational, and early. Proof should sit near the decision, not after it.
- Story personality: fast, familiar, and job-relevant. Stories are for time-bound opportunity, not self-expression at scale.
- Employer personality: real, responsive, and accountable.
- Candidate personality: ambitious, practical, and proof-oriented.
