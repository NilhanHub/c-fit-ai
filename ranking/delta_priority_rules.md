# Delta Priority Rules

- Walk-ins opening soon and deadlines closing soon outrank passive updates.
- Status movement outranks generic newness when the employer is trusted and active.
- Salary disclosure and trust upgrades move jobs upward because clarity increases actionability.
- Suspicious-listing downgrades still surface as trust events, but they do not compete with safe roles in the main feed.
- Digest-ready events stay lowest because they are useful but less urgent.
