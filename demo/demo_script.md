# Demo Script

1. Open the landing state and show the language switch plus role cluster selection.
2. Point out the preview card that already frames the core promise: fresh jobs, urgent stories, and status changes.
3. Enter the pulse and show the “what changed since your last check” header.
4. Scroll the story reel and explain that stories are not generic content; they are urgent, job-linked actions.
5. Open the top-ranked Jetwing job and point out trust cues, salary disclosure, freshness, and status rail.
6. Toggle `Verified only` and `Salary shown` to demonstrate trust-first filtering.
7. Open the candidate story composer and show the `3 Reasons to Hire Me` template.
8. Open the employer urgent-story composer and show the walk-in / intake template.
9. Open the report sheet to demonstrate that suspicious content is actionable and moderation-aware.
10. Close with the trust pillars and explain why this beats a prettier job board.
